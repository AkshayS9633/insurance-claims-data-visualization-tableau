## Overview
Data visualization project using Tableau to analyze car insurance claims and uncover patterns related to risk, customer behavior, and claim frequency.

## Business Objective
Help senior management make data-driven decisions by identifying trends in claims, high-risk customer segments, and factors impacting claim amounts.

## Data
Car insurance claims data covering customer demographics, income, vehicle details, usage type, claim frequency, and claim amounts.

## Approach
- Explored claims data to identify trends and anomalies  
- Built interactive dashboards to analyze demographics, vehicle types, and claim behavior  
- Created a storyboard summarizing insights and business recommendations  

## Tools & Skills
 data visualization, exploratory data analysis, business storytelling.

## Key Insights
- Older drivers and newer vehicles show higher claim amounts  
- SUVs contribute the highest claim frequency and value  
- Education and occupation strongly influence claim behavior  
- A small group of high-value claimants drives a large share of total claims
